Subject: Introducing WeClaw: Open source AI for WeChat group protection

---

We shipped WeClaw.

It's an AI-powered agent that scans WeChat groups for scam and fraud. Runs on your desktop. You approve every removal. 100% open source.

**The problem**
Manual moderation doesn't scale. Scammers slip in, post links, impersonate brands. By the time you notice, damage is done. We spent months watching group admins burn 1–3 hours a week just scanning.

**The approach**
Vision-based automation. No brittle APIs. WeClaw sees the WeChat UI like you do—screenshots, LLM, structured prompts. It proposes removals. You confirm. It executes. Human-in-the-loop by design.

**Why open source**
Transparency. Extensibility. Your data stays yours. We're building what the community asks for—reply generation, friend request verification, and more.
