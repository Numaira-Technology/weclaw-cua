How does WeClaw actually detect scams in WeChat groups?

5 steps. Vision-based. Human in the loop.

Thread 👇

--- TWEET 2/6 ---
1. CLASSIFY

The agent scans your chat list and labels each thread: group vs. individual. Only groups matter for scam detection. Skips DMs.

--- TWEET 3/6 ---
2. FILTER UNREAD

Focuses on unread group chats. No point re-scanning groups you already cleared. Saves time + API calls.

--- TWEET 4/6 ---
3. READ & EXTRACT

Opens each unread group, takes a screenshot, sends to the LLM. Extracts suspects: spam links, phishing language, impersonation, fake giveaways.

Pattern matching + context. Not keyword blacklists.

--- TWEET 5/6 ---
4. BUILD PLAN

Creates a removal plan: who to remove, why. Presents it to *you*.

You approve or cancel. No auto-removal. Ever.

--- TWEET 6/6 ---
5. EXECUTE (with verification)

Agent clicks through the WeChat UI. After each action, a fast verification model confirms success. Find → Click → Verify. Loop until done.

That's it. 5 steps. Your machine. Your data. Open source.
