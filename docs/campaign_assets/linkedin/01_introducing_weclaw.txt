Title: Introducing WeClaw: Open source AI for WeChat group protection

---

Copy (paste into LinkedIn post):

WeChat group admins: how much time do you spend scanning for scams, spam, and suspicious accounts?

We built something for that.

Introducing WeClaw — an open-source, AI-powered agent that scans WeChat groups for fraud and scam activity. It runs on your desktop, uses vision-based automation (no brittle APIs), and asks you to confirm before removing anyone.

What it does today:
✓ Classifies and filters group chats
✓ Reads messages with AI to detect suspicious patterns
✓ Proposes removals — you approve, it executes
✓ Extensible via skills (markdown playbooks, no code changes)
✓ Works standalone or with OpenClaw

Why open source?
Transparency. Community. Your data stays on your machine. We're building in public and inviting contributors to shape what comes next—reply generation, friend request verification, and more.

If you moderate WeChat groups or care about messaging security, we'd love your feedback.
